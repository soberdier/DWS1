HTML Definitions

<h1>

The main heading

<h2>

The level 2 heading

<h3>

The level 3 heading

<h4>

The level 4 heading

<h5>

The level 5 heading

<h6>

The level 6 heading

<p>

paragraph text

<ul>

a bullet list

<ol>

a numbered list

<li>

list item

<strong>

indicates strong importance

<em>

indicates emphasis

<blockquote>

indicates a paragraph of quoted text

<q>

indicates a short quote in the paragraph

<abbr>

indicates an abbreviation or acronym

<cite>

indicates a citation

<dfn>

indicates the defining instance of a new term

<address>

indicates the contact information for the author of the site

<ins>

indicates that content has been inserted

<del>

indicates content has been deleted

<s>

indicates that content is no longer accurate
